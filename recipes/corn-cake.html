Chef Mike's Famous Corn Cake

Description

A quick cake recipe that can be altered for many flavours.

Ingredients

  • 190g whole eggs
  • 20g egg yolks
  • 80g white sugar
  • 1.5 salt
  • 75g corn juice
  • 40g melted butter
  • 30g flour

Process

  1. In bowl whisk together all eggs, then whisk in remaining ingredients until blended and smooth.
  2. Pass through fine mesh.
  3. Transfer batter to canister of whipped cream charger. Charge with 2 NO carts, shake hard.
  4. Invert canister and shoot into 250ml plastic cup. Immedietly place in microwave and heat at 90% power for 40 seconds.
  5. Turn cup upside down and tap to dislodge cake. Repeat with remaining batter
