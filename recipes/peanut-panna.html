Peanut Panna Cotta

Description

A richly flavoured peanut pannacotta with a light creamy texture. Perfect capstone to an intense entree.

Ingredients

  • 270g whole milk
  • 150g pure peanut butter
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 105g white sugar
  • 5g salt
  • 4.3g iota carrageenan (0.9%)

Process

  1. In a blender, combine the milk, vanilla, sugar, and salt.
  2. While blending, slowly sprinkle in the carrageenan to disperse.
  3. Pour mixture into saucepan, then bring to boil on stove.
  4. Whisk in peanut butter until thoroughly combined, then pour into cups or molds.
  5. Let cool and leave to set in fridge.
