Grapefruit Souffle

Description

A grapefruit flavoured souffle, can be substituted for any other citrus. Bright ending for a summer patio dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250g grapefruit juice, zest of grapefruit half
  • 100g white sugar
  • 15g cornstarch
  • 30g water
  • 2 egg whites (~70g)

Process

  1. Combine juice, half the sugar, and zest in saucepan over med-low heat. Simmer until reduced to 150ml then remove the zest.
  2. Combine cornstarch and water. Whisk into hot juice and continue whisking over low until thickened.
  3. Submerge pan in cold bath, whisk until mixture is cold (~3min).
  4. Whip eggwhites until soft peaks, then add remaining sugar and whip until stiff peaks.
  5. Fold eggwhites into juice gel in two batches.
  6. Divide into 6 ramekins or grapefruit halves (dust inside pith with sugar). Bake at 160*C for 15 mins or until puffed/browned.
